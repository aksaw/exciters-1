D1 is an exploration of a new grammar of composition informed by the laws that govern bodies in space.

We’re weaving the motions of nature and machines into the digital soundstream that circulates through silicon veins.

Exciters enables you to trigger notes with gravity. You can also loop phrases and yield unusual rhythms.

Attractors enables you to create chords using planetary motion.

Barcode lets you generate staccato rhythms with mouse strokes.

D1 works by sending MIDI directly into your DAW. You can pipe the MIDI into any synth or sampler.

Open this page on your desktop browser to use D1 with your DAW.
